What is Advanced Collagen Plus?

Advanced Collagen Plus is a daily collagen powder marketed to adults looking to support skin elasticity, joint comfort, and stronger hair and nails as natural collagen production slows with age. Rather than a single collagen source, it combines multiple collagen types from different sources into one unflavored powder that's designed to dissolve into hot or cold beverages.

It's positioned as a daily-routine supplement rather than a fast-acting one — the manufacturer describes it as working gradually with consistent use over several weeks, which is typical of how collagen peptide supplements are generally marketed.

Ingredients

According to product listings, Advanced Collagen Plus is built around a blend of the following:

  • Collagen Types I & III (bovine) — sourced from grass-fed bovine collagen; these are the most abundant collagen types in the human body and are commonly associated with skin structure and elasticity in collagen supplements generally.
  • Collagen Type II (chicken) — sourced from pasture-raised chicken collagen; often included in joint-focused formulas alongside cartilage-support ingredients.
  • Collagen Type V (marine/fish) — sourced from wild-caught fish; frequently paired with bone- and connective-tissue-support claims.
  • Collagen Type X (eggshell membrane) — sourced from organic eggshell membrane; typically included to round out a multi-type blend.
  • Biotin — a B-vitamin commonly added to hair- and nail-support formulas.
  • Chondroitin Sulfate — a cartilage component commonly included in joint-comfort formulas.

Note: these are general descriptions of what each ingredient category is commonly used for in collagen supplements — they are not confirmed clinical outcomes for this specific finished product, and none of these ingredients are a substitute for medical evaluation of a skin, joint, or connective-tissue condition.

How to use it

The typical directions call for one to two servings daily, stirred into hot or cold water, coffee, or another beverage. As with most collagen peptide products, it's described as unflavored and designed to dissolve without altering taste or texture.

Reported timelines vary from person to person: some users report noticing changes within a few weeks, while manufacturers of collagen powders generally suggest 4–8 weeks of consistent daily use before expecting more noticeable results. Individual outcomes are not guaranteed and depend on factors like age, baseline collagen levels, diet, and overall health.

Safety & considerations

Advanced Collagen Plus is marketed as generally well tolerated by healthy adults when used as directed. Mild digestive discomfort is occasionally reported with collagen peptide powders and can often be minimized by taking the product with food and water.

As with any supplement, people who are pregnant or nursing, managing a medical condition, taking prescription medication, or allergic to any of the listed animal-derived ingredients (bovine, chicken, fish, or egg) should speak with a healthcare professional before use. Persistent joint pain, skin changes, or hair/nail changes can sometimes signal an underlying condition that's worth having evaluated by a doctor rather than self-treating with a supplement alone.
